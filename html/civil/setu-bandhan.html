EVENT TYPE : Team  
EVENT DESCRIPTION: Aspiring to be a Civil engineer or trying to feel like one??? Then here is your chance Yes you can make your own bridge with only ice cream sticks and glue Paridhi 2022 brings to you another chance to test your skills, so what are you waiting for?? Pull up your socks and make the best bridge so that it can take the load of your fattest friend THIS EVENT IS OPEN FOR ALL.

RULES & REGULATIONS:

  • The minimum span of the bridge must be 50cm, the minimum width (internal) is 5cm and the vertical height must be a minimal of half of the Popsicle stick.
  • 200 Popsicle sticks & 2 cups glue will be provided. Threads and clips can also be used participants can bring it if required. (WHICH HAS TO BE REMOVED AT THE TIME OF LOAD TESTING)
  • Only truss bridge and Under-arch Bridge is allowed.
  • The participants will be given 3 hours for construction, 1 hour for decoration and after that load testing will be done (Colours and brushes should be brought by the participants). Prior to the load testing, viva will be taken by the teachers.
  • Final result will be calculated on load testing efficiency, viva and aesthetics.
  • Participants must not bring any extra items. If found, participants will be disqualified. Decision of the co-ordinator will be final.
