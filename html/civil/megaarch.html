EVENT TYPE : Team  
EVENT DESCRIPTION: Wanna get some real life experience with various bridge then surly be a part of MEGA ARCH where you can make bridge with bricks and sand Paridhi 2022 brings to you Chances to win your pocket money just by making bridges that you all made in your childhood with cards. THIS EVENT IS OPEN FOR ALL.

RULES & REGULATIONS:

  • All the members of the team will be provided with a fixed no. of bricks(bricks for load testing not included in this) and a fixed amount of sand.
  • Teams will have to construct the arch in the time span of 2 hours, if the time limit exceeds the team will be disqualified.
  • The inner distance of the arch must be 60 cm. If found less, respective team will be disqualified.
  • The height of the keystone from the inner edge of the brick must be below 3 cm, if found more the arch bridge won’t be valid.
  • Only keystone remains in contact with the bricks to be used for laod testing.
Game Play : Max of 5 members.
REGISTRATION FEES: 40 ₹
SCORES: Scoring will be available on D-Day.
IN CASE OF ANY DISPUTES, THE DECISION OF THE EVENT COORDINATOR WILL BE FINAL.
