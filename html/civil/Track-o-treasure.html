TRACK-O-TREASURE

Some quick example text to build on the card title and make up the bulk of the card's content.

EVENT TYPE : Team  
EVENT DESCRIPTION: If solving tricky yet easy sums in a minute is your hobby then come and join us in Paridhi 2022 and be a part of Track-o-treasure. All you need is a measuring tape and a mobile compass that will be provided. Just come and play to discover what's hidden for you at the end.

RULES & REGULATIONS:

  • Each team will be provided with a mobile compass and a measuring tape. Any other measuring instruments will NOT be allowed in the event.
  • Participants/Teams have to bring their own calculators/cell phones for calculations related to measurement or solving numerical questions.
  • Different paths/routes will be allotted to different teams that are to be followed in order to reach their respective finishing points.
  • There will be certain number of "station points" each path. The distance and bearing (ANGLE) of the next station point will be disclosed by solving riddles/ numerical given at that station point.
  • Hints will be provided after a certain period of time. If the teams are unable to solve the riddle at a given station point at the cost of a particular time penalty as decided by the coordinator (fixed for every team).
  • The team reaching the finishing point in the least time will be declared as winner.
  • Word puzzle set would be chosen via a lottery system. Each set will contain 15 questions where hints for first 10 questions would be given and hints for the remaining 5 questions would carry bonus points if team members find them without hints.
Game Play : Max of 3 members.
