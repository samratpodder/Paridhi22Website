ROBO CLASH
EVENT TYPE : Robowar 
EVENT DESCRIPTION: Robo-clash is one of most exciting events of Paridhi in which two participants fight with their bots. In this event the participants have to design a robot using great technical effort and customized tools to fight with their opponents and to prove your worth in the battle field.

RULES & REGULATIONS:

  • The dimension of the bot must be 40cm x 40cm x 40cm, including wheels (+10% tolerance is allowed).
  •  The weight of the bot must not exceed 5 kgs.
  • Maximum voltage used for driving the bot should be 20V- 20Amp. Toggle is allowed.
  • The team should consist of maximum 4 members.
  • Match will be played between 2 teams. The team which wins will go to the next round.
  • Weapons are strictly not allowed as bot modification.
  • Point systems will be introduced at the time of start of the event.
  • Participant must not misbehave or abuse any other participants or coordinators. This can be taken seriously and lead to disqualification from all the events.
  • Any Team can re-register multiple times if they want to with same or different bots.
Game Play : 4 members
REGISTRATION FEES: ₹500
SCORES: Scoring will be available on D-Day.
IN CASE OF ANY DISPUTES, THE DECISION OF THE EVENT COORDINATOR WILL BE FINAL.
