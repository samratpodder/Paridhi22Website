SQUID GAME
EVENT TYPE : On Spot (Timebased) 
EVENT DESCRIPTION: Wanna have some actions with your bot and make instant money? We bring to you the "Squid Game". In this first of it's kind robotics event you get to race, crash and speed your way to victory with the winner getting exploits on spot. But beware, it isn’t a normal "Game", it’s the epic “Battle of Life". Tune up your skills so you can go hard or just go home.

RULES & REGULATIONS:

  • It is an on-spot, one round event in which three participants will compete against each other with the same bot but one at a time.
  • The bots will be provided to the participants.
  • The participant who completes the whole track in minimum time will be declared as winner.
  • Winners will get the cash prize instantly.
  • No technical timeout will be given.
  • In case of any deliberate or accidental damage to the arena, the team will be immediately disqualified.
  • During the race, any hand touch will cause penalty. However, if the bot touches the arena walls, there will be no penalty.
Game Play : individual
REGISTRATION FEES: ₹50
SCORES: Scoring will be available on D-Day.
IN CASE OF ANY DISPUTES, THE DECISION OF THE EVENT COORDINATOR WILL BE FINAL.
