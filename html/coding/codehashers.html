EVENT TYPE : Individual  
EVENT DESCRIPTION: A strong developer will be in demand for the foreseeable future! And Coding is the basic requirement to develop yourself. Here we present an event, Code Hashers for you guys to showcase your programming skills. It is all year coding competition.It's all about understanding your opponent's code. It's 2V2 competition.It contains team of 2 member. Here you can solve interesting Programming and earn cash prizes!So come with your partner and showcase your talents in Paridhi.

RULES & REGULATIONS:

  • The event is an on-spot type event. The team members will be allowed a short time to decide who starts off with which PC. Each of the 2 members will start solving their respective problems with the start of the timer. On the notification of Half-Time the two coders would be asked to switch their PC and continue where their partner has left. The team which finishes the given problem set the fastest would be judged as the Winner and remaining places would be calculated accordingly.
  • Draw Scenario: In case, both solve the same no. of test cases (zero included) then 2 options can be presented to the participants:
  • Both can walk out without any cash as prize money.
  • In case, they re-register they get Rs. 30/- cash back
  • A Team can participate in any one of the Paridhi days within the allotted time
