CHESS

EVENT TYPE : Single (Onspot)  
EVENT DESCRIPTION: Chess is a game of strategy and skill. The event is a platform for you to showcase your skills in Chess and earn your bragging rights.

RULES & REGULATIONS:

  • A checkmate will lead to a direct win.
  • In case of stalemate or no result after 30 minutes the decision will be taken by summing up the points of the remaining troops for each of the participants in that match.
  • Each player will get 15 seconds to play his turn each time which will be maintained by a stopwatch.
  • After the allotted time for each player the turn will automatically go to the opponent.
