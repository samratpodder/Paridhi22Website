ELECTRO SPARK

EVENT TYPE : Maximum 2  
EVENT DESCRIPTION: A three rounds event consisting of word search, circuit completion and riddle number combination.

RULES & REGULATIONS:

  • The event will be conducted in three rounds.
  • The first round will be the elimination round where all the teams will be provided with a word search puzzle individually and they’ve to solve the given.
  • Word puzzle set would be chosen via a lottery system. Each set will contain 15 questions where hints for first 10 questions would be given and hints for the remaining 5 questions would carry bonus points if team members find them without hints.
  • Qualifying to the 2nd round would be on the basis of time and the score of individual teams in round 1.
  • The 2nd round would be the circuit design round where they’ve to complete an incomplete circuit with required logic gates to get the desired output.
  • All the participants of 2nd round would be qualified to the 3rd round.
  • In the final round, teams will have two options where they will either randomly guess the given combination of four digits manually or they will solve riddles corresponding to the digits and get the correct combination.
  • Teams will get the options according to their performance in round 2.
  • Participants solving with riddles will get 3 chances but the participants opting to guess the combination will get unlimited opportunities in 15 minutes.
  • Winners will be decided on the basis of required time along with their performance in the previous two rounds.
Game Play : 2 members
REGISTRATION FEES: ₹ 60
SCORES: Scoring will be available on D-Day.
IN CASE OF ANY DISPUTES, THE DECISION OF THE EVENT COORDINATOR WILL BE FINAL.
