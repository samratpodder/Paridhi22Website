ABOUT US
Megatronix is known to organize one of the best technical fests of Kolkata, PARIDHI, the First Version of which was held on 17th and 18th March 2012. With an all total of 41 events, the Sixth Edition of the Three-Day Annual Inter-College Technical Festival, Paridhi 2017-18 set a whole new level in terms of success and popularity amongst students throughout the city and beyond. It is one of the biggest fest of Eastern India with participation from all across India. This year we are hosting the 10th edition of Paridhi with an elegant array of events. Hope to see you soon on campus.
